# YouTube

Google, the owner of YouTube, allegedly used YouTube videos to train AI services without the creators' consent. Although, aparently they weren't the only ones. For more information, refer to [Google is training its AI tools on YouTube videos: These creators aren't happy](https://techxplore.com/news/2025-08-google-ai-tools-youtube-videos.html).

To check if your YouTube videos have been used to train AI, refer to [Search the YouTube Videos Secretly Powering Generative AI](https://www.proofnews.org/youtube-ai-search/)

For more information about the use of AI on YouTube, refer to [Your data and using AI features on YouTube](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/14772770?hl=en).
