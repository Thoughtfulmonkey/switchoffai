# Adobe Acrobat Reader

Free PDF reader and gateway to paid-for PDF editing tools.

## Turning off generative AI features

To turn off generative AI features, complete the following steps:

1. Open Adobe Acrobat Reader.
2. Choose one of the following options to access the **Preferences** panel:
    * In the upper-left corner of the application, select **Menu**, and then select **Preferences**.
    * Press Ctrl + K.
3. From the **Categories** list, select **Generative AI**.
4. On the **Generative AI features in Acrobat** page, clear the **Enable generative AI features in ** checkbox.
5. Select **OK**.
6. Close and reopen Adobe Acrobat Reader.
