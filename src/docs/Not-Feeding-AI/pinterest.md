# Pinterest

A social media site where you create collections of images, videos, websites, and other internet content.

## Preventing your data from being used to train AI 

Complete the following steps:

1. Go to [https://ie.pinterest.com/settings/privacy/](https://ie.pinterest.com/settings/privacy/).
2. In the **GenAI** section, turn off **Use your data to train Pinterest Canvas**.

If you can't use the previous steps to go directly to the privacy settings page, complete the following steps instead:

1. Go to https://pinterest.com/. The site might redirect you to a regional version.
2. Select the gear icon (**Settings & Support**) in the lower-left corner of the page.
3. Select **Settings** from the menu that appears.
4. Select **Privacy and data**.
5. In the **GenAI** section, turn off **Use your data to train Pinterest Canvas**.

For more information, refer to [Manage GenAI settings](https://help.pinterest.com/en-gb/article/manage-genai-settings) in the Pinterest Help Centre.
