# X (aka Twitter)

The social media site that the Financial Times referred to as ["the deepfake porn site formerly known as Twitter"](https://www.ft.com/content/ad94db4c-95a0-4c65-bd8d-3b43e1251091).

## Preventing others from editing your images

Anyone with an X account can use AI to edit any other user's images. The feature was added on December 24, 2025. 

* To prevent other users from being able to edit your images with AI, save them in the gif format before you upload them. Most image editors are able to save images as gifs, such as Microsoft Paint. Typically, you select **File** > **Save as**, and then choose the gif image format.

!!! important "Important"
    X could allow users to edit gif images in a future update. 

## Preventing the sharing of your data

To ask X not to share your data with other companies, or use it to train their AI chatbot, Grok, complete the following steps:

1. Go to [x.com](https://x.com/).
2. From the menu on the left-hand side, select **More** and then select **Settings and privacy**.
3. From the **Settings** menu, select **Privacy and safety**.
4. Scroll down the page to the **Data sharing and personalization** section.
5. Select **Grok & Third-party Collaborators**.
6. In the **Grok & Third-party Collaborators** panel, clear all checkboxes.

## Leaving X

The safest way to protect your data, although with no guarantees, is to close your X account. 

### Downloading your data

Complete the following steps to download your posts, uploaded media, and other data:

!!! note "Note" 
    It might take over 24 hours before the data is ready to download.

1. Go to [x.com](https://x.com/).
2. From the menu on the left-hand side, select **More** and then select **Settings and privacy**.
3. From the **Settings** menu, select **Privacy and safety**.
4. In the **Your Account** panel, select **Download an archive of your data**.
5. In the **Verify your password** panel, enter your password, and then select **Next**.
6. In the **X data** section, select **Request data**.
7. When you receive an email that says your archive is ready, select the link or complete steps 1 to 6 to return to the archive page. The email is likely to take over 24 hours to arrive. 
8. Select **Download archive**.
9. Your data might be divided into separate downloads. Select **Download archive** for each one.

### Deactivating your account

Complete the following steps to deactivate your account:

!!! note "Note" 
    Your account will become unavailable on X immediately, but your it will not be fully deactivated for 30 days.

1. Go to [x.com](https://x.com/).
2. From the menu on the left-hand side, select **More** and then select **Settings and privacy**.
3. From the **Settings** menu, select **Privacy and safety**.
4. In the **Your Account** panel, select **Deactivate your account**.
5. Select **Deactivate**.
6. Enter your password, and then select **Deactivate account**.
