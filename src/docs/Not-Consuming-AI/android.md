# Android

The operating system used on many phones and tablets.

There's a wide variety of device configurations and customised versions of Android. So, unfortunately, the instructions on this page aren't guarenteed to work for your device.

## Turning off the virtual assistant shortcut

On many Android devices, if you hold the power button for a few seconds, you start the virtual assistant. Most recently, this is Google's AI agent, Gemini.

To change what happens when you hold the power button, complete the following steps:

1. Go to your phone's settings. You might have a **Settings** app. Alternatively, swipe down from the top-right corner of your device, and then select the gear icon. You might need to swipe down twice.
2. Search for each of the settings listed in the table. If you see an exact match, select the search result, and the follow the additional steps:

    | Setting                         | Additional steps                                                              |
    | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
    | gesture shortcuts               | Select **Launch Google Assistant**, and then turn off the setting.            |
    | button navigation               | Turn off the **Hold Home for Assistant** setting.                             |
    | long press                      | Select **Long press**, and then select **Power off menu**.                    |
    | Press and hold the power button | Select the **Power menu** option instead of the **Digital assistant** option. |

## Uninstall Gemini

Gemini is Google's AI app that replaces Google Assistant. It might be preinstalled on your device or have been installed as part of a system update. You can uninstall it in the same way that you would any other app. If you're unsure how to do that on your device, try the following:

1. Go to your phone's settings. You might have a **Settings** app. Alternatively, swipe down from the top-right corner of your device, and then select the gear icon. You might need to swipe down twice.
2. Tap **Apps**.
3. Tap **Manage apps**.
4. In the list of installed apps, tap **Gemini**.
5. On the **App info** page, tap **Uninstall**.
6. In the confirmation prompt, tap **ok*.
