# Facebook

This page explains how to prevent your posts on Facebook from being used to feed AI.

**Warning: You need to opt out.**

Meta, who own Facebook, state that they "use public information on Meta Products to develop and improve generative AI models for our AI at Meta features and experiences." To prevent Meta from using your public information to feed AI, you need to submit an objection.

# How to submit an objection

2. Go to https://www.facebook.com/privacy/genai
3. Scroll down to and expand the **Your right to object** section.
4. Select **Learn more and submit requests here**.
5. on the **Data subject rights for third-party information used for AI at Meta** page, select the checkbox.
6. Complete the form that appears.

**Note:** You must enter at least 25 characters in the **Relevant prompts** field, even if you haven't used the Meta AI chatbot. You must also provide an explanation in the **Additional context** field.

7. Select **Send**. This site sends a code to your email address to verify your identity.
8. Copy the copy from the email into the **Confirm your email address** panel, and then select **Confirm**.
