# Apple iOS

The operating system for Apple phones.

## Turning off Apple Intelligence

Apple Intelligence was added in iOS 26. It added artificial intelligence features to many of the apps. The instructions to turn it off are available at the bottom of the [Use Apple Intelligence on your iPhone](https://support.apple.com/en-ie/guide/iphone/iphc28624b81/ios) page, and are reproduced here:

* Go to the **Settings** app, tap **Apple Intelligence & Siri**, then tap the button next to **Apple Intelligence**.
